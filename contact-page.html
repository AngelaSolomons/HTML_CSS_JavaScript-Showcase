Get to Know Us

Why Choose Us?

Our tutors will work together with students, parents, and teachers in order to create and reach personal educational goals.

Sessions are scheduled at times convenient for you. Our tutors can meet in the most comfortable environment: the student's home, school, coffee shop or even online.

One-on-one tutoring focused on the material you need to succeed. Sessions go at your pace and provide the comfort to ask questions and receive corrections.

Our Founders

IMAGE OF
GROUP

TutorLink was founded by five students who knew firsthand how hard it is to find affordable, trustworthy tutoring that actually fits your course and learning style. Frustrated by expensive options and impersonal matching, they built TutorLink to connect tertiary students with tutors who have already aced the same classes.

Our Mission

"To help students achieve academic success by connecting them with trusted and affordable tutors."
